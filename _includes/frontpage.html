My principal research interests are in the area of Software Engineering, Machine Learning, and Data Mining. While a wide range of operations could be done in each one of these fields, as a bachelor student, I did not have enough time to get highly deep into each of them. So, I tried some disciplines.

Being a Software Engineering trainee in one of the top universities of Iran encouraged me to practice in different sections of Software Engineering. I have done some studies in Cloud Computing inclouding Workflow Scheduling and Resource Allocation with Meta-Heuristic methods at the Cloud Computing Lab under the Supv of Dr. Abrishami. Besides, as a professional technical lead and software engineer in Fanap Co., I am working on a cloud platform and cloud storage which made me investigate distributed systems. Additionally, I have been a teacher assistant in the software engineering lab for more than a year researching open-source software and games' architecture and design under the Supv of Dr. Rasoolzadegan.

Data mining is the second field on my list. Working with Dr. Behshid Behkamal on geospatial data, National Post's data and passing the Data Mining course interested me in this field. I worked on geo-location data for almost two years as a member of WTLab with Dr. Pourmasoumi and Dr. Behkamal. During this time, I worked as a part of the team working on an industrial project for the National Post company. This project proposed to predict the parcel delivery time based on the current system and find the bottlenecks in the current system with process mining and Machine Learning techniques. Currently, I'm working as a Research assistant On Knowledge Grapgs and NED/NEL under the supervision of Dr. Davood Rafiei.

Jun. 2018 – Apr. 2021 | Software Engineer - Technical Lead

SAKKU Cloud Platform, is a Docker container-based cloud platform! As a founder and technical lead in Sakku, my duty was to:
  • Coordinate a development team of 9 developers and 2 DevOps engineers,
  • Design the architecture of the project with a Microservice approach,
  • And develop the modules using Spring Framework and libraries.
During the first phase, we used Apache Marathon, Mesos, Docker Swarm, and many other technologies. We achieved to save 70\% of resource usage and 310 users were engaged to develop, deploy, and maintain their applications. Today, Sakku manages 3 clusters, +1K CPU cores, 2.5T Memory, and ~450 Services for Fanap Co. Infrastructure team.

JThumbnail, is a Java library for creating Thumbnails of common types of file! I developed this project for using in different services of Podium like PODSpace or Talk.
